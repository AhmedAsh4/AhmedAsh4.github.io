Aquanaut

Extraordinary Rounded Octagonal Case

The Aquanaut showcases a unique rounded octagonal case design, crafted from stainless steel. Its sporty yet refined appearance, coupled with a combination of brushed and polished finishes, offers a contemporary take on luxury sports watches, echoing the iconic design language of the collection.

Innovative Dial with Embossed Patterns

The dial of the Stainless Steel Aquanaut is designed for optimal legibility. Its embossed pattern, reminiscent of the Aquanaut's iconic texture, harmonizes with the strap. The luminous hands and Arabic numerals ensure easy readability in various lighting conditions.

Textured "Tropical" Strap and Clasp

The watch features the signature "Tropical" strap, made from a high-tech composite material that's resistant to wear, water, and UV radiation. The embossed texture resembles the pattern of the dial, creating a seamless aesthetic. The deployant clasp ensures a secure and comfortable fit on the wrist.
