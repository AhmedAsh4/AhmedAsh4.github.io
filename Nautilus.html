Nautilus

Iconic Porthole Design

The Nautilus is renowned for its distinctive porthole-inspired design, featuring an octagonal bezel and rounded case shape in stainless steel. This iconic aesthetic, designed by Gerald Genta, exudes a sporty yet sophisticated appeal, setting it apart in the realm of luxury sports watches.

Coveted Collectability

The Stainless Steel Nautilus holds a unique place in the world of luxury watches due to its scarcity and desirability. Its limited availability and high demand create an aura of exclusivity, making it not just a timepiece but a coveted collector's item. The waitlists and fervor surrounding its acquisition emphasize its status as a luxury icon.

High-Quality Movement and Craftsmanship

Housing Aurora Watches' caliber 324 SC movement, the Nautilus ensures exceptional accuracy and reliability. The meticulous finishing and attention to detail in the movement represent the brand's dedication to horological excellence, visible through the sapphire crystal case back.
